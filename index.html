Naetag is a dedicated platform to help sellers by featuring business to business cross promotion where the sellers themself are the partners rather the being competitors. We enabled sellers to promote each other strategically and the customer gets the most out of it.

Why us ?

Do you have a service or a product ? If so, How do you promote? Print media, digital media or hired an ad agency who does everything for you. But, How do you track the customer you get v/s the money you have spent on promotion ? Hence, our platform is there with a different and intresting way of promotion. Our main objective is to empower sellers which ultimately lead to customer benifit. When the customer get cashback or points, They buy only from the sellers they wish . But, On Naetag, we give them coupons/deals from other sellers that makes every sellers on our platform to get equal exposure. Our high level of data analytics helps us to unserstand customer behaviour and accourdingly we provide coupons/deals from different sellers partner which has high potential to convert.
